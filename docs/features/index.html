Features

We build the project so Qalingo meet standard requirements for eCommerce projects needs a limitless customization.


The data model allows you to customize your product depending on the country or target region catalog (eg Europe).

Catalog

  •  Categories and subcategories Unlimited
  •  Groups unlimited attributes (sizes, colors ...)
  •  Unlimited versions (red, green , S , M, L ...)
  •  Variations Unlimited products (eg red t-shirt , XL)
  •  Features unlimited products
  •  Pictures unlimited
  •  Multiple pictures per product
  •  Product Reviews by customers
  •  rating of products by customers
  •  Categories restricted to groups of customers
  •  Documents attached to sheets ( references ... )
  •  Gift Lists ( wishlists )
  •  Management manufacturers , brands
  •  Related Products: cross selling : cross products , up selling : upgrading

Customers

  •  Customer registration and personal account
  •  Recovering lost password
  •  Order tracking by e -mail by Client
  •  Ability to create client groups
  •  Backup baskets created
  •  Subscription newsletter and opt-in
  •  Storage baskets created by clients ( abandoned carts )
  •  Revival of customers who have not ordered for a preset time
  •  E- mails to the customer in case of replenishment of a product

Orders

  •  Status order configurable
  •  Order, Invoices and delivery notes PDF
  •  Gift packaging billable

Shipping mode

  •  Carriers and unlimited destinations
  •  Carriers assigned to specific zones or states
  •  Notification by e-mail delivery statuses (customizable)
  •  Ability to track the parcel by parcel number
  •  Delivery addresses or separate billing

Translation, i18n

  •  Translations current default in 7 languages​​: English, French, Spanish, German, Italian, Dutch, Portuguese, Japanese, Chinese traditional Chinese simplied, Vietnamese

Localisation

  •  Unlimited number of currencies
  •  Formatting currency
  •  Add unlimited taxes
  •  Taxes and country or area
  •  Taxes by State

SEO

  •  Optimisation (SEO)
  •  Management Meta tags (Meta title, keywords ...)
  •  canonical URL (in progress)
  •  Google Sitemap (in progress)
  •  simplified URL
  •  Permalinks (client wishlist)

Cache Engine

  •  Motor cache to enhance performance
  •  Trigger cache by AOP
